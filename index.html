About

Dr. Ran He is a Professor in the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), which belongs to Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). He received his Ph.D. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from CASIA in 2009. His research thrusts include the area of information theoretic learning, computer vision, image processing, pattern recognition, and machine learning.

Recruitment

We are recruiting Assistant Professors, Postdoctors, Algorithmic Engineers, Doctors, Masters and Interns.
Anyone who really likes to do research on artificial intelligence is welcomed! You are required to master a certain foundation in machine learning or computer vision research and have great passion for research. I am looking forward to working with you!
